# Code of Conduct

ABC4RD Academy is committed to a respectful, professional, beginner-friendly learning environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Discuss ideas, sources, and technical accuracy.
- Welcome beginners without lowering standards for accuracy.
- Assume good intent, but correct mistakes clearly.
- Credit public work and original authors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Spam, mass tagging, or promotional flooding.
- Presenting unverified relationships as facts.
- Using a person's name, talk, project, or organization as endorsement without consent.
- Sharing private contact information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove issues, pull requests, comments, or discussions that violate these expectations.

## Disclaimer

ABC4RD Academy is an independent educational initiative. Unless explicitly stated, it is not officially affiliated with Bitcoin Magazine, BTC Inc, BTCB.tech, or any referenced conference, company, speaker, sponsor, or organization. Public references are used for educational research, attribution, and community learning.
